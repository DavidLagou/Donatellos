True Italian Fine Dining

Donatello’s greatest goal was to bring true authentic Italian cuisine to Vancouver, British Columbia. In the process of creating this goal, many unique and original dishes had been created throughout the menu in pastas, pizzas, appetizers and more. Our pastas are made with rich Mario Batali Alfredo sauce or creamy Gia Russa Alla Vodka sauce. We really want our guests to taste a bit of Italy with every bite.

Unique Dishes Created in House

For all of our new guests, we recommend asking your server what their favourite dish is; but ultimately you cannot go wrong with any dish from Donatellos. With fresh organic vegetables from local farms across British Columbia, pizza dough hand stretched and created in house, and cooked to perfection in our fire-stone oven. Donatello’s has various food alternatives for those who have allergies and dietary restrictions so that the whole party can find a dish that they enjoy.

Rich and Crisp Wines

The main asset of Donatello’s wine is its true force. All wines in houses are located all across Italy. Our most popular wines are located just outside the Barolo town limits, on the road to Monforte. These wines are all produced in Barolo, Langhe Nebbiolo, Langhe Freisa, Dolcetto d'Alba, Barbera d'Alba, Rosae. Donatello’s. Leaving a rich and savory sense in your mouth with various bodies to choose from. Strong aromas from our older wines and juicy bouquet from our younger ones.
